Designer | Developer | Creator

Welcome! My name is Kaylee Horner, a designer, developer, and creator. I have a B.S. from Kansas State University (Go Cats) in Apparel Marketing and a minor in Business Administration. Wait, what?? Apparel, fashion? You read that right. However, as time has gone on it has become clear that I am meant for coding, content, graphic design, and writing. Can I find a way to do it all? We will have to see.

I grew up teaching myself how to use programs like Paint, GIMP, HTML and CSS coding, to ran a "button /graphics" page on Neopets when I was in middle school. In high school I moved on to the Adobe Suite and I haven't looked back since. Post-college, I have taken on a coding bootcamp to expand my coding knowledge past the basics. Through this course I have learned the front end, including Javascript and currently am learning the baack end.

When I'm not working my day job or working through the bootcamp, I like updating my Spotify playlists, keep up-to-date with sports, and watch anything that will make me laugh. If you need anything at all, don't hesitate to contact me using the contact link -- I'd love to hear from ya! Enjoy scrolling through what projects I've working on and feel free to stay awhile.
